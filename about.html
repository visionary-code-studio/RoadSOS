🚨 RoadSOS+ — Reimagining Road Safety & Emergency Response
RoadSOS+ is an intelligent, offline-first emergency road assistance platform engineered to prevent avoidable road deaths. Our absolute priority is the Golden Hour — the critical first 60 minutes after a crash when immediate medical intervention increases survival odds by over 80%.
🎯 Our Mission & Operational Impact
In developing nations like India, road accidents claim over 150,000 lives annually. A substantial portion of these fatalities occurs because bystanders are paralyzed by panic, emergency services receive inaccurate coordinates, or cellular coverage drops on national highways.

RoadSOS+ addresses these core failure modes through a dual-channel design system that bridges victims, bystanders, and local authorities seamlessly, even in low or zero-bandwidth environments.
⏱️ The Golden Hour Operational Timeline
Minute 0 - 1
Accident Occurs & SOS Fired
The victim or bystander hits the SOS button. The app instantly locks the high-accuracy GPS coordinates using hardware GPS receivers.
Minute 1 - 2
Multi-Channel Emergency Beacon
A detailed emergency packet is broadcasted via HTTP. Simultaneously, a pre-formatted SMS fallback containing the Google Maps GPS location is dispatched to trusted contacts.
Minute 2 - 5
OSM Services Mapping
The browser geolocates all surrounding trauma centers, police stations, and mechanic shops using Nominatim and OpenStreetMap Overpass queries, plotting them on an interactive Leaflet map.
Minute 5 - 15
Interactive AI First Aid guidance
While emergency responders are en route, the Gemini-powered AI Guide acts as a calm first aid dispatcher, guiding the bystander step-by-step through CPR, bleeding management, and shock control.
Minute 15 - 60
Rescue & Golden Hour Care
Responders navigate precisely using live GPS data. Commuters are warned of road hazards via Firebase push alerts. The patient is delivered safely to trauma care within the Golden Hour.
⚙️ High-Tech Technical Architecture
RoadSOS+ is built to be resilient, lightning fast, and offline-resilient. Here is a breakdown of our advanced architectural layers:
LAYER TECHNOLOGIES & PROTOCOLS FUNCTION
Core Frontend HTML5 · CSS3 Glassmorphism · Vanilla JS · Three.js · Leaflet.js Vibrant glassmorphic interface, interactive 3D traffic simulator, and responsive maps.
API Backend Python FastAPI · Uvicorn · OSM Overpass API · Nominatim Serves live nearby emergency infrastructure and high-speed geocoding.
AI Engine Google Gemini 2.0 Flash (Primary) · Claude Sonnet (Fallback) Calm, concise, multi-lingual emergency response and CPR instructions.
Resiliency Layer Service Workers · LocalStorage database · SMS Fallback protocol Guarantees full capability offline (cached maps, offline AI Guide rules, offline helplines).
Alert Engine Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) · Push Protocol Broadcasts background push alerts to drivers in active accident zones.
